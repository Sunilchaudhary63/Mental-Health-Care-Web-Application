Hospitals

Chandan hospital

Chandan Hospital is a 24-hour, fully-equipped medical centre, which is equipped to handle accidents, heart attacks, strokes, breathlessness, burn and all other emergencies. The Institute of Emergency and Trauma care is a state-of-the-art medical care centre, with the latest medical equipment.

Nirvan hospital

For over 35 years, Nirvan has been more than just a hospital in Northern India; it’s a sanctuary for healing. We offer expert care for a wide range of mental health challenges, from anxiety to addiction.

Medanta hospital

Medanta Lucknow is one of the largest multi-super-specialty hospitals in Uttar Pradesh. Built with the aim to provide highest standard of tertiary healthcare within the state and to the neighbouring regions.

Mental Patient

Mental well-being is vital, yet quality care remains inaccessible for many due to stigma, cost, and distance. To address this, a Mental Health Care Web Application offers affordable, accessible, and secure support. Using AI and ML, it features empathetic chatbots, sentiment analysis, online therapy, self-help content, and anonymous emotional sharing. Prioritizing privacy and available 24/7, it complements traditional therapy and aims to support marginalized and rural communities.
